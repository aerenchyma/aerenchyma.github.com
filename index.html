The word aerenchyma refers to spaces or air channels in the leaves, stems and roots of some plants, which provide a low-resistance internal pathway for the exchange of gases such as oxygen and ethylene between the plant above the water and the submerged tissues [from Wikipedia] (I’m not a trained biologist, but the world is pretty neat).
